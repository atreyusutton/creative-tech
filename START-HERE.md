
## 💡 Pro Tips

### Documentation Best Practices
- **Document as you go** - Don't wait until the end!
- **Show your process** - Include failures and iterations
- **Explain everything** - What, how, and why for all media
- **Use quality media** - Good photos make a huge difference

### Site Management
- **Commit often** - Save your work regularly
- **Test locally** - Always check before deploying
- **Keep organized** - Use consistent naming for files
- **Update regularly** - Add content throughout the semester
